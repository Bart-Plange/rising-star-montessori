What Makes Us RISMOS

Rising Star Montessori School was started in the year 2009. The School began with just a handful of students with Mrs Kootin as the first teacher. The school was mainly preschool and for children from 6 months.

Why Choose Us

Serene Environment

Rising star boasts of great infrastructure and a serene environment. We have a modern baseketball court, a summer hut that accomodates the learners during closing and a spacious and neat canteen.

Well Trained & Dedicated Teachers

Our Teachers are well trained and very qualified for the job. Almost all our teachers are graduants from different universities accross the country.

Mode of studies and Delivery of Academic Work

Mode of studies and deliviery is learner centered and thus accounting for our outstanding performances over the years.

Modern style & Classrooms

Our classrooms are well organized, well ventilated and stuffed with all TLMs to assist both Facilitators and Learners.

Pre-school to Junior High

RISMOS has four departments: the pre-school, Lower Primry, upper primary and Junior High.

Well Equipped Library and ICT Lab

The school boasts of a modern library stacked with all kinds of reading materials appropriate for both learners and teachers. Our ICT laboratory is one of a kind with full set computers and projector.

OUR CORE PRIORITIES

There are three main arms the school functions by
These are our core priorities: Our Vision, Our Mission and Our Core Values

OUR MISSION

Provide a carefully planned stimulating environment which will help children develop within themselves the foundation for a life-time of learning, thinking and living

VISION

To nature, educate and provide guidance to the children in the right direction to develop courage an belief in themselves to continue to meet life's challenges

CORE VALUES

  • To develop sense of high esteem
  • To cultivate inner disciplne, interdependence and a sence of pride
  • To develop and foster a sense of respect for oneself, for others and for the world
  • To develop habit of intuition and persistence
