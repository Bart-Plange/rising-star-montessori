Frequently Asked Questions

These are mostly asked questions and their answers provided concerning the school

  • Does RISMOS perform well in their final exams?

    Yes, RISMOS has choked up many successes in the past and continues to do so. In 2021, we presented 35 candidates for the exams. 22 students had aggregrates from 06 to 09 with the least aggregrates being 13.

  • Does RISMOS follow the New Standard Based Curriculum for Schools?

    The Standard Based Curriculum provided by GES encapsulates all schools in Ghana and RISMOS is not an exception. So yes, our curriculum is solely based on the New Curriculum. We do not undertake any foreign curriculum.

  • How can my child get to school if our distance is relatively far from the school?

    Distance has never been a problem for RISMOS students. There are seven buses and counting to convey learners in and out of school. Currently, our learners are based mostly in the Greater Accra and Eastern regions and there are buses to cater for all.

  • Are RISMOS learners disciplined?

    Even though our current society shows a low level of discipline in children, RISMOS has a setup team that ensures and caters for discipline and obedience to rules and regulations.

  • Does RISMOS accept wards with disabilities?

    In terms of disabilities, call our administrators to explain the level of severity.
